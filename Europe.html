Scenic Places arround Europe

ROME, ITALY:
Rome hosts the Cinecittà Studios, the largest film and television production facility in continental Europe and the centre of the Italian cinema, where many of today's biggest box office hits are filmed. Rome has a sovereign state located entirely in its city limits, the Vatican City, which is also the world’s smallest state. Famous for it's Gorgeous Fountains, Roman ruins, wine, history, architecture and culture. There are beautiful places to visit here as:

  • St. Peter's Basilica
  • Sistine Chapel
  • Pantheon
  • Roman Colosseum
  • Trevi Fountain
June, July, and August see the heaviest tourist traffic in Rome because many people take their holidays in the summer during the school break.

LONDON, UK:
London is one of the leading tourist destinations in the world and in 2015 was ranked as the most visited city in the world with over 65 million visits.There are beautiful places to visit here as:

  • London Eye
  • Buckingham Palace
  • British Museum
  • St Paul's Cathedral
  • The View from The Shard
Spring: March to May. Visiting London in the spring, you’ll enjoy longer daylight hours and mild weather.
Summer: June to August. The summer is a great time to visit, with warm (even hot!) weather.
Autumn: September to November. The busy summer season comes to an end.
Winter: December to February. Although London is colder in winter, average temperatures of between 2 and 6°C (36 to 43°F) mean you’ll still be able to fully enjoy the city ...

PARIS, FRANCE:
It is one of the most visited cities in the world. Paris was one of the first large European cities to use gas street lighting on a grand scale on its boulevards and monuments.There are beautiful places to visit here as:

  • Louvre
  • Eiffel Tower
  • Palace of Versailles
  • Disneyland
  • Musee Picasso
January. With the festivity and fun of the holiday season still in the air, there’s no better way to start January than on the stunningly elegant Champs-Élysées in Paris

VENICE, ITALY:
Venice, Italy, is known by several names, one of which is the ‘Floating City’. This is due to the fact that the city of Venice consists of 118 small islands connected by numerous canals and bridges. There are beautiful places to visit here as:

  • St Mark's Basilica
  • Rialto Bridge
  • Santa Maria e San Donato
  • Monastery of Sao Nicolo al Lido
  • Grand Canal
Taking all of that into account, the best time to visit Venice is November. If you want to have the city to yourself, it's best to visit in the off-season.

Germany:
Germany was the first country in the world to adopt Daylight saving time – DST, also known as summer time. The Berlin Zoo, which opened in 1844, is the oldest in Germany, and claims the most comprehensive collection of species in the world.There are beautiful places to visit here as:

  • Saxon Switzerland national Park
  • Schwerin Castle
  • Zugspitze
  • Neuschwanstein
  • Brandenburg Gate
There really is no bad time to visit Germany, but the best time to visit Germany is May when the weather has finally warmed.
